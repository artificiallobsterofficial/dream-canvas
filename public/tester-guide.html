Beta Tester Guide · ~15 min + a day-2 visit

✨ Welcome to DreamCanvas

DreamCanvas is a digital vision board — the corkboard of goals, quotes, and habit trackers people hang at home, rebuilt as a web app. You're one of its first testers. Your job: use it like it's yours, and tell us where it shines or breaks.

⚠️ Three things to know before you start

  • Your board lives in this browser on this device. A different device, browser, or incognito window starts a fresh board — and clearing your browser data erases it. The download icon (top right) exports a backup file.
  • The AI coach is running in offline mode for this test — it gives canned (but pleasant) responses. Judge the boards, trackers, and dashboard, not the AI's wit.
  • Desktop is the main experience. On a phone, the app opens in Focus mode by design — canvas editing on touch is a known rough edge. Phone testers: focus on Focus mode.

1Build your board (~10 min)

  1. Pick a template — left toolbar → Templates. Choose whichever fits your life.
  2. Make it yours — drag things around, resize them (bottom-right handle), and edit the text. Click any item to open its style panel: try changing a color, a font, and the rotation slider.
  3. Add three things from the + menu: a Habit Tracker for a real habit you're working on, a Sticky Note with a real goal, and an image (paste a URL or upload from your device).
  4. Add stickers — decorate until it feels like yours.
  5. Mark today done on your habit tracker (click today's date → Done).
  6. Move around — drag empty canvas to pan, Ctrl + scroll (or the bottom-right buttons) to zoom. Try to "lose" an item far off-screen, then find it again. Could you?

📲 On a phone? Install it first

DreamCanvas works as an app: iPhone — in Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Android — tap the install banner or menu → Install app. Installed, it works offline and can send daily habit reminders (enable them in Settings ⚙️).

2Try the other modes (~3 min)

  • Focus mode — toggle at the top of the header. Check off a habit, write one line in the journal, look at your XP. Is it clear what this screen is for?
  • Zen mode — the eye icon in the top-right corner of the canvas. Just the vibes, no buttons.
  • Backup — click the download icon in the header and confirm you got a file.

3Come back tomorrow (~2 min)

  • Is your board exactly how you left it?
  • Mark your habit done again — after 3 days in a row you should see a 🔥 streak badge appear.
  • Be honest: did you want to come back?

4Bonus missions (optional, for the thorough)

  • Try to break it. Paste a huge wall of text into a note. Add 30 stickers. Zoom all the way out. Spam-click things.
  • Export your board, clear it (trash icon), then re-import the file. Did everything come back?
  • Upload a large photo from your device. Did it work and look right?
  • Resize your browser window very narrow. Does anything look broken?

📝 Then tell us (the important part)

Send your answers to whoever gave you this link — voice memo, text, whatever is easiest:

  1. What confused you, even for a second? (The #1 most useful answer.)
  2. What broke, looked wrong, or did something you didn't expect?
  3. What did you genuinely like?
  4. Would you actually use this weekly? Why or why not?
  5. What's the one missing thing that would make you use it?
💡 Notes while you go beat a summary at the end — the moment something feels off, write it down.
